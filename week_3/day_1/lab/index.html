Robyn Rihanna Fenty

Humble Beginnings

Robyn Rihanna Fenty was born on February 20th, 1988 in Saint Michael, Barbados. She grew up in Bridgetown, Barbados. At 16 she dropped out of high school. One year after dropping out, she moved to America and signed a deal with music label Def Jam records.

Music Awards and World Record Holder

Rihanna's talent throughout her career has earned her eight Grammy Awards, twelve Billboard Music Awards, nine American Music Awards, and four iHeartRadio Music Awards. She has also broken multiple Guinness World records including:

  • Most Consecutive years of UK no.1 singles
  • Female artist with most U.S no. 1 singles in a year
  • Most digital no.1 singles in the US.
